The Basics The basic communication format is OFNR Observation – objective descriptions of the situation Feeling – identify the feeling of the individual Need – identify the need of the individual Request – a clear actionable request It also requires a calm mindset, so that you can stick to just these 4 elements What to Say In the game, when you hear an emotional statement, you will be required to provide the correct feeling or need word. You may press the ‘Easy Mode’ button to toggle a list of common responses to support you. Also, you can use the ‘clue’ button to get a specific clue for the answer. Why it works NVC works because showing a person that you understand their feelings and needs resonates on an instinctive level with them. It says to them, you are one of the herd, and can be trusted.
